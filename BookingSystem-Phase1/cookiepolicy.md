# Cookie Policy

## 1. Cookies Used
The booking system uses essential session cookies only.

## 2. Purpose of Cookies
Cookies are used to:
- Maintain user login sessions
- Ensure secure authentication

## 3. No Tracking Cookies
The system does not use analytics, marketing, or third-party cookies.

## 4. Legal Basis
Essential cookies are used based on legitimate interest and contract performance.  
Consent is not required under GDPR.

## 5. Cookie Retention
Session cookies are deleted automatically when the browser is closed.

## 6. Managing Cookies
Users may disable cookies in their browser settings.  
Disabling cookies may prevent the service from functioning correctly.
